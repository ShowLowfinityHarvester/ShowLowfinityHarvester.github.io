GTA 5 Is Now Released, Long Lines At Gamestop On San Mateo Blvd!
Jayson Doutree Sep 17, 2013
We woke up really early this morning to go order the new Grand Theft Auto Game for our PS3 this morning, and the lines were busy!
We waited for a really long time to get this game! I've never seen a gamestop this busy before for anything. People even slept overnight just to get this game. Rockstar must be making great money with how crazy it all is here.
And all the hype it is getting is sooooo worth it. this is a way better title than the last game. The graphics have been improved significantly and the overall gameplay is just smooth. The radio stations also sound really good. I wonder if that paramore song is on there? I don't care if I'm 5 years old at this point I'm still playing it! So What are you waiting for? Go to your nearest gamestop and try out this game if you haven't already!
