Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at WSLF Radio. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information when you visit our site or use our services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users automatically. There are no data trackers, cookies, or analytics tools in use on our website. The only information we collect is what you voluntarily provide through forms, such as song requests, ban appeals, or other submissions.

How We Use Information

The information you provide is used solely for the purpose of fulfilling your request, such as processing song requests or addressing ban appeals. We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

WSLF Radio does not use any third-party services that collect data from our users. Any external links on our site may lead to sites with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before providing information.

Data Retention

Information submitted through forms is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of the request. Once it is no longer needed, the data is securely deleted.

Your Rights

If you have submitted information to us and wish to have it deleted or inquire about what data we have, you can contact us via the appropriate communication channel provided on our site.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Please review this page periodically for updates. Valid (12/09/24)
