Weather Synopsis (Product #2; Done After Station ID):

Header - Here is the latest weather synopsis.

NOTE: Ever since Hurricane Hilary in August 2023, during major severe weather threats (Usually for at least an SPC MRGL Risk and/or WPC MDT Risk), LOX would not read the actual synopsis text but rather the Short and Long Term forecasts. The weather synopsis header will still be used for this occasion. The part that says “.SHORT TERM (WDY-WDY)...DD/HMM XM.” would not be read but the part that says “.LONG TERM (WDY-WDY)...DD/HMM XM.” would be read. Sometimes, but very rarely, during the threat for conditions like gale force winds in the waters, this same occasion would happen but with the MARINE section of the Area Forecast Discussion. The part that says “.MARINE…DD/HMM XM.” will not be read.
